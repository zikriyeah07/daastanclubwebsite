What is Daastan CLub?
Daastan is a company that operates with two main sub-bodies: Daastan Tours and Travel and Moments by Daastan.
What is Daastan Tours & Travel?
Daastan Tours and Travel specializes in offering a wide range of travel services, including customized tours, travel packages, and travel-related experiences for individuals and groups. It aims to provide seamless and unforgettable travel experiences for its clients.
What is Moments By Daastan?
Moments by Daastan specializes in photography and event management. They capture the essence of events through professional photography while also handling the complete management of events. Whether it's a wedding, corporate gathering, or personal celebration, Moments by Daastan ensures a memorable experience by managing every aspect of the event and preserving the moments through stunning photography.
Can I customize my travel package or event services?
Yes, both Daastan Tours and Travel and Moments by Daastan offer customized packages. You can discuss your specific preferences with the team, and they will tailor the services to suit your needs.
